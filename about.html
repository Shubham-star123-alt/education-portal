About Us

This website was developed by Shubham Shatrughna Panchal as part of an educational and social initiative aimed at improving awareness about government schemes, educational resources, and support systems available for schools and students.

The core idea behind this project came from our group: Himesh Bhosale, Pranav Panchal, Aditya Prakash Yadav, and Sahil Bhosale. Together, we visited several government schools in and around our region to understand real-world challenges faced by students, teachers, and school administrations.

During our visits, we observed that many schools struggle with proper communication with government departments, awareness of schemes, access to funding opportunities, and information about student support programs. Many beneficial schemes exist, but the lack of centralized information often prevents schools and students from taking advantage of them.

This website aims to bridge that gap by bringing together government schemes, scholarship opportunities, educational resources, complaint portals, and institutional support information in one accessible place.
